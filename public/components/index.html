A few things everyone can do now

Numerous people contribute to the project to varying extents, but the chief architects are Nick Mathewson and Roger Dingledine.

  • Consider running a relay to help the Tor network grow.
  • Take a moment to donate to support further Tor development.
  • Help translate the documentation into other languages.
